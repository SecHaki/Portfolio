# 👋 Welcome to My Portfolio!

## About Me
I'm a cybersecurity professional with hands-on experience supporting enterprise SOC and systems operations for a large public-sector organization. I work daily with SIEM, EDR, endpoint management, and vulnerability management tools to detect threats, harden infrastructure, and support secure systems implementation across thousands of endpoints and users.

My background combines formal cybersecurity education with real-world defensive operations and infrastructure work — threat hunting, log analysis, security automation, endpoint hardening aligned with CIS and CJIS standards, and large-scale device management modernization. I enjoy turning raw security data into actionable insights, whether that's through Splunk dashboards, PowerShell automation, or improving detection and response workflows.

I'm actively building and documenting projects in my GitHub portfolio to demonstrate practical security skills, continuous learning, and a strong blue-team mindset, with a focus on SOC operations, systems analysis, and cyber defense.
